Instruction

Task Description

In this task, you will be paired with another participant and engage in a multi-session conversation. Before each session, you will be provided with a simulated time gap and some events that may have occurred during that gap. Your task is to have a conversation with your partner, taking into account the events and time gap that have been provided.

Instructions:
1. You will be matched with another participant and will have several sessions together.
2. Before each session, you will be given a simulated time gap (e.g. 1 week, 1 month, etc.) and a list of events that may have occurred during that time.
3. Use the information provided to continue the conversation with your partner, taking into account the events and time gap that have occurred.
4. Be respectful and polite to your partner at all times.

Requirements:
1. Must be able to communicate in English fluently.
2. Must be able to commit to at least 2 sessions with your partner.
3. Must be able to follow instructions and engage in a respectful and polite conversation with your partner.

Task steps:

Step 1: Match

When you are ready for the task, hit the Start Matching button at the bottom of this page.

Step 2: Confirm your initial events

After redirected to the chat room, you will need to confirm your initial event. You could use this inital event as your current status (we also encourage to use your own initial events that descript your status).

Step 3: Have the conversation

You can input your text in the Input Area and hit the Post button to send your messages. Your messages can NOT be deleted once sent.

Step 4: Start a new session

Each session require at least one turn from each participant. Both you and your task partner can start a new session at any time by hitting the New Session button. Each conversation should have 2 to 7 sessions.

Step 5: Confirm the new events

In a new session, you will find new time gap and events. You will then need to continue the conversation as if you have experienced the time gap in reality.

Repeat step 3 to 5 until the end of the conversation.

Step Payment

To get paid, you need to submit your HIT by clicking the Finish the HIT and Go Back to AMT after the conversation.
